# Task 2: Why

## Part 1: Tasks as target-action pairs

**Q1: Does an athlete need to have certain bodily attributes (like height and weight) to be successful in certain sports?**
- **Action:** Compare / Query
- **Target:** Correlation / Attributes
- **Description:** We investigate the relationship between Height/Weight and Success (Medals) within specific Winter sports.

**Q2: Are there sports that are dominated by certain teams?**
- **Action:** Summarize / Compare
- **Target:** Distribution / Outliers
- **Description:** We map teams against sports to identify disproportionate success (dominance) in specific categories.

**Q3: Are certain sports more popular for men or women in certain teams?**
- **Action:** Compare / Query
- **Target:** Distribution / Trends
- **Description:** We compare participation and success across Gender and Team for each Winter sport.

## Part 2: Interaction Intent

To answer these questions effectively, we implement:
1. **Manipulation:** Highlighting specific data points or categories on hover to see exact values.
2. **Faceting:** Partitioning the data by clicking on Heatmap cells to update the Scatter Plot view.
3. **Reducing:** Filtering the dataset (e.g., by Gender or Medal type) using interactive visual marks.
