### Marks and Channels

#### Parallel Coordinates

The marks are connection marks (polylines). The primary channel used is vertical spatial position on aligned scales to encode the quantitative attribute values.

#### Parallel Sets

The marks are areas (ribbons/bands). The key channel is width, which encodes the frequency or count of items in each category.

#### Your Chosen Time Visualization

The time visualization is a multi-line chart where the marks are connection marks linking point marks. The primary channels are horizontal spatial position (time) and vertical spatial position (temperature).
