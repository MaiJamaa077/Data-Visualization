### Task 2: Why

#### Part 1: Tasks as target-action pairs

**Q1: Which teams are best at athletics and did this change over time?**

- Action: Summarize / Compare
- Target: Distribution / Trends
- Description: We want to summarize the medal count per team to identify the "best" and track these trends over time to see shifts in dominance.

**Q2: Does the nationality and the location of the games have an influence on a player's performance?**

- Action: Compare / Query
- Target: Correlation
- Description: We compare a team's performance (medal count) in their home country versus foreign locations to identify potential "home advantage" correlations.

#### Part 2: Questions to the data

1. **How has gender participation in athletics evolved over the decades?** 
   - *Action: Identify / Target: Trends.* This helps understand the history of inclusivity in the games.
2. **Which athletic events produce the most repeat medalists?**
   - *Action: Discover / Target: Outliers.* This investigates if certain sports favor career longevity more than others.
